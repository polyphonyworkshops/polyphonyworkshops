When:

our workshops are taking place:

  • as blocks of workshops in the duration of a term
  • as a one-off crush course during half terms and holidays
  • as one-off events upon request of 3 or more people

Where:

Online has proven to be the easiest way to bring people together.
It also gives the opportunity to use interactive digital content which makes lessons more fun.

Our Goals:

  • Offer students a general understanding of music.
  • Offer students the chance to meet and interact with other music students of their age with mutual interests.
  • Offer students the experience and opportunity to get to know different paths of music science.
  • Prepare students for theory exams and other exam components without taking time from their performance lesson.
